About Sri Nanda Electricals

At Sri Nanda Electricals, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and installing high-quality electrical control panels for industries and commercial enterprises. With over a decade of experience, our mission is to deliver reliable, efficient, and safe electrical solutions that help businesses operate smoothly and safely.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians works closely with clients to understand their requirements and provide tailor-made solutions, including:

  • PCC Panels (Power Control Centre)
  • MCC Panels (Motor Control Centre)
  • PLC Automation Panels
  • Custom Control Panels
  • Panel Installation & Maintenance

We pride ourselves on using top-quality components, adhering to international standards, and ensuring every project is completed on time. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of numerous industries across the region.
